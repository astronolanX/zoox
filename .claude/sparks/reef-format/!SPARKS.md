## Thread Synthesis

The thread is converging on a counterintuitive insight: optimizing the *syntax* (sigils, tags, delimiters) may be solving the wrong problem. The real question is whether explicit metadata is necessary at all, or whether AI can infer type, scope, and lifecycle from content and structure.

Open tensions:
- Explicit vs implicit metadata
- Human-readable vs AI-optimal
- Structure vs prose
